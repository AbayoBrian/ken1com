ABOUT US

Our Story

Sanai Holdings Ltd is a general merchandise supplies company founded in 2014. Our main focus is effective and efficient provision of general merchandise and supplies to both private and government sectors.

We pool together the efforts of young minds with great interpersonal skills and versatility to perform duties. Our impressive solutions aim at provision of a wide variety of goods and services.

Located at Kenya House Complex, Koinange Street, Nairobi, we ensure effective management and coordination of all activities from sourcing, packaging, and delivery to monitoring communication with customers.

WHO WE ARE

Our Mission

We are an Agricultural Consultancy and Technology firm with key strengths in Irrigation, Water and Greenhouse technologies.

Our mission is transforming farms and livelihoods through provision of timely, accessible and affordable information, services and technologies.

YOUTH AND WOMEN AGENDA

We are committed to training and supporting the youth to become astute agribusiness investors, farm management and support staff, greenhouse technicians and irrigation technicians.

We actively support equal participation of women in agribusiness through targeted programs and initiatives.

Our Value Proposition

  • Systematic and holistic approach to delivering farm establishment and management solutions.

  • Creative and out of the box approaches to promoting sustainable and profitable agribusinesses.

  • Technical Support and Market Linkages for our clients.

  • Agricultural Information dissemination and coaching through various platforms.
